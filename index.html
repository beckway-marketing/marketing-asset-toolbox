How to Use This Hub

  1. Search first. Hit the Overview tab and type what you're looking for. Most things live here already.
  2. Browse by Practice or Industry if you're not sure of the exact name.
  3. Drop new content into the Pipeline tab. The hub auto-tags it. Click to fix anything that's wrong.
  4. Anything marked "Needs Review" hasn't been touched in over a year. Open it, confirm it's still accurate, and mark it reviewed.
  5. Can't find what you need? Submit a Marketing Request from the Quick Actions above.

Best Practices for Sharing Externally

  • Confirm the asset is current. If it's flagged for review, don't send it out until Marketing confirms.
  • Check with the owner if you're unsure whether something is approved for external use.
  • Use the latest brand templates. Older formats may not match the 2026 standards.
  • No off-brand colors or fonts. Stick to the palette and typography above.
  • When in doubt, reach out to Christine in Marketing. Better to ask than to send a stale or off-brand asset.
